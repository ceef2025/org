Game Master Handbook (Rules & Execution)

1. Chinese Whispers with Dance

Objective:

To successfully convey a dance move whispered to the first person through non-verbal physical performance to the last person in the chain.

Execution:

  • Divide 3 teams of 8-10 people. Teams line up side-by-side.
  • MC whispers the dance name (e.g., 'Macarena', 'Worm', 'T-Rex') to the first person.
  • The first person TAPS the second person, who turns around. The first person must perform the move for 10 seconds.
  • The second person attempts to replicate the move for the third person, and so on.
  • The last person in line must guess the original dance move name aloud to the MC.
  • **Winner:** The team that correctly guesses or comes closest to the original move wins.

2. Head, Shoulder, Toe Challenge

Objective:

To grab the water bottle before the opponent when the keyword "Bottle" is called, despite following conflicting commands.

Execution:

  • Contestants (pairs) face each other across a small water bottle placed on the ground.
  • MC calls out body parts: "Head," "Shoulder," "Knee," "Toe." Contestants must touch the called part.
  • When the MC calls out "BOTTLE," contestants race to grab the water bottle first.
  • The winner stays and challenges a new opponent.
  • **Winner:** The person who wins the most rounds (or the last person standing, depending on time).
