Game Master Handbook (Rules & Execution)

GAME 1: Chinese Whispers with Dance (7:50 PM)

Execution Refined:

  • **Teams & Setup:** 3 teams, 8-10 players each, lined up facing away from the stage.
  • **Action:** Host Shivani shows the sealed dance card to the *last* person, who must perform the move *non-verbally* to the person in front.
  • **Winner Status:** No official winners for this game. Focus on team cooperation and fun!
  • **Demo Link:** Video Demo

INTERMISSION: Scavenger Hunt 1 (8:05 PM) - 2 x Silver Prizes

Execution:

  • **Action:** Host calls out common but random objects. **2 Questions / 2 Winners.**
  • **Winning:** First 2 people to bring the item to the Host win a **Silver** prize.

GAME 2: Head, Shoulder, Toe Challenge (8:10 PM) - 3 x Coral Prizes

Execution Refined:

  • **Setup:** Pairs of contestants. Place one water bottle between them.
  • **Action:** Host calls out body parts. Grab the **"BOTTLE"** when called.
  • **Winners:** **3 Rounds / 3 Winners** of **Coral** Tier prizes.
  • **Demo Link:** Video Demo

INTERMISSION: Scavenger Hunt 2 (8:25 PM) - 2 x Silver Prizes

Execution:

  • **Action:** Host asks for 2 more random items. **2 Questions / 2 Winners.**
  • **Winning:** First 2 audience members to present the item win a **Silver** prize.

GAME 3: The Balloon Race (8:30 PM) - 3 x Coral Prizes

Execution Refined:

  • **Setup:** 3 race lanes. 5 racers per race. Hold a balloon between feet.
  • **Action:** Race to the point and back without dropping/touching the balloon with hands.
  • **Winners:** **3 Rounds / 3 Winners** of **Coral** Tier prizes.

INTERMISSION: Scavenger Hunt 3 (8:45 PM) - 2 x Silver Prizes

Execution:

  • **Action:** Host asks for the final 2 random items. **2 Questions / 2 Winners.**
  • **Winning:** First 2 audience members to present the item win a **Silver** prize.
