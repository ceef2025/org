üé≤ Games & Activities Description

Chinese Whispers with Dance

The MC whispers a dance move/style to the first person. They must act/dance it out for the next person, who then relays their interpretation. The last person guesses the original move. (Materials: None)

Head, Shoulder, Toe Challenge

Teams race to grab a water bottle (placed a few feet away) only when the MC calls out "Bottle." (Materials: Water Bottles)

The Balloon Relay

Team relay race where participants must jump-walk while handling or passing a balloon using only their feet. No hands allowed. (Materials: Balloons)

The CEEF Ramp Walk

Individuals or small groups are invited to walk the "ramp" and showcase their confidence and attire. (Materials: Good music track for a runway)
